## What Mindoo Is

Mindoo is not a productivity app.
Mindoo is a life operating system.

It takes a person who feels lost, stuck, overwhelmed, and unclear — and systematically helps them understand themselves, find their direction, remove their blockers, and build a life of freedom and purpose.

## The Core Problem Mindoo Solves

Modern life creates cognitive fragmentation:
- Too many inputs, zero unified system
- Chronic open-loop anxiety (things not captured)
- No self-knowledge system — people do not know who they are
- No clarity on direction, purpose, or next step
- Cognitive performance never trained or monitored
- Blockers never identified or eliminated systematically

## The Seven Principles (Never Change)

1. **Science-based always** — every recommendation citable to real research
2. **Personal always** — every answer traceable to the user's real data
3. **Never crashes** — error boundaries and failovers everywhere
4. **Modular always** — adding a module never touches existing modules
5. **Data-safe always** — user data is never lost or corrupted
6. **Transparent AI** — the AI shows its reasoning, never hallucinates
7. **Progressive** — the system gets smarter the more it is used

## The AI System

### Failover Chain
1. Primary: Claude (Anthropic) — best quality, deep reasoning
2. Fallback 1: Groq (llama-3.3-70b) — fast, free, capable
3. Fallback 2: OpenRouter — routes to best available model

3. **The Planner** — turns goals into actionable steps
4. **The Guardian** — monitors chaos, energy, emotion, alerts proactively
