# 05 — ABOUT ME PROFILE
## The Self-Discovery Foundation
**Last Updated**: April 5, 2026
**Status**: Design Phase — Not yet built

---

## Philosophy

The About Me profile is not a form to fill in once.
It is a living document that gets richer over time through:
- Direct answers to guided questions
- AI conversations that extract insights
- Behavioural patterns inferred from platform usage
- Periodic review and deepening

The AI uses this profile as the foundation for every personalised response.

---

## Profile Sections

### Section 1 — Current Situation
Questions asked once, updated as life changes:
- What is your current employment status?
- What do you do for work (or what did you do most recently)?
- What is your approximate monthly income range?
- Where do you live and what is your living situation?
- What are the 3 biggest constraints in your life right now?
- What does your ideal day look like vs your actual day?

**AI uses this to**: Understand your starting point and real constraints before any advice.

---

### Section 2 — Personality & Working Style
- How do you prefer to work — alone, in a team, or a mix?
- How do you make decisions — with logic and analysis, or gut and intuition?
- How do you communicate best — written, verbal, visual?
- When you learn something new, what helps most — watching, reading, doing, listening?
- Describe yourself in 5 words. Be honest, not aspirational.
- What do people who know you well say about you?

**AI uses this to**: Match advice to your actual personality, not an ideal version.

---

### Section 3 — Energy Patterns
- What time of day do you feel sharpest and most creative?
- When do you typically crash or feel foggy?
- What drains your energy fastest?
- What restores your energy most reliably?
- How many hours of sleep do you function best on?
- Does your energy vary by day of the week?

**AI uses this to**: Schedule recommendations around your actual energy, not generic advice.

---

### Section 4 — Passions & Genuine Interests
- What topics do you read about when nobody is watching?
- What could you talk about for hours without getting bored?
- What did you love doing as a child before anyone told you what to be?
- What skills do you have that feel effortless to you but hard for others?
- What skills do you wish you had? What draws you to them?

**AI uses this to**: Find the intersection between passion and potential income paths.

---

### Section 5 — Values Hierarchy
- List your top 5 values (freedom, family, creativity, security, etc.)
- When you have to choose between two things you want, what wins?
- What would you refuse to do even if it paid very well?
- What would you do even if it paid nothing?

**AI uses this to**: Ensure all recommendations align with what actually matters to you.

---

### Section 6 — Ikigai Mapping
Four questions that reveal your life purpose intersection:
- What do you LOVE doing? (passion)
- What are you GOOD AT? (vocation)
- What does the WORLD NEED that you can provide? (mission)
- What can you be PAID FOR? (profession)

**AI generates**: Your personal Ikigai statement and most aligned paths.
**Science base**: Ikigai concept (Mogi), purpose research (Damon), self-determination theory (Deci & Ryan)

---

### Section 7 — Financial Picture
- What is your current financial situation honestly?
- What is your monthly income target for feeling free?
- What is your relationship with money like?
- What financial decisions do you consistently get wrong?
- What financial blockers are holding you back?
- What is the most you have ever earned and how?

**AI uses this to**: Find your most realistic and aligned path to financial freedom based on your Ikigai and skills.

---

### Section 8 — Relationships & Support System
- Who are the 3 people who energise and support you most?
- Who consistently drains your energy?
- Do you have a mentor or someone you learn from?
- What kind of support do you wish you had that you do not?
- What is your relationship with asking for help?

**AI uses this to**: Identify your real support network and gaps, and make relational recommendations.

---

### Section 9 — Dreams & Fears
- If you had no constraints (money, skills, time), what would you build or do?
- What does freedom look like for you specifically?
- What does success look like for you specifically?
- What is the thing you most want but are most afraid to pursue?
- What would you regret not trying?

**AI uses this to**: Keep your recommendations anchored to your real vision, not generic success.

---

### Section 10 — Blockers (Multi-Level Audit)
The most important section. Honest answers unlock real progress.

**Mental blockers**:
- What limiting beliefs do you know you hold?
- What stories do you tell yourself about why you cannot do something?
- Where do you consistently self-sabotage?

**Psychological blockers**:
- What fears stop you from taking action?
- What past experiences still affect your decisions today?
- What triggers your anxiety or avoidance?

**Financial blockers**:
- What would you do differently if money was not a constraint?
- What financial situation is currently blocking your progress?

**Physical blockers**:
- What physical limitations or health challenges affect your productivity?
- How does your body feel on a typical day?

**Relational blockers**:
- Is there a relationship that consistently holds you back?
- Do you have people around you who believe in your vision?

**AI uses this to**: Build a personalised blocker elimination plan. Science base: ACT (Hayes), CBT (Beck), motivational interviewing (Miller & Rollnick)

---

### Section 11 — Cognitive Self-Assessment
- Do you have any known learning difficulties?
- How is your memory? Strong, average, or weak?
- How long can you focus before your mind wanders?
- What subjects or skills have you always found difficult to learn?
- What are you currently trying to learn and what is blocking you?

**AI uses this to**: Build a personalised cognitive training programme.

---

## How the Profile Builds Over Time

### Phase 1 — Quick Start (5 minutes)
Answer 3 questions from Section 1 and 3 questions from Section 4.
Enough for the AI to give basic personalisation.

### Phase 2 — Foundation (20 minutes)
Complete Sections 1, 2, 3, 4, 5.
AI now gives properly personalised responses.

### Phase 3 — Deep Profile (45 minutes)
Complete all sections.
AI now has full context for life-changing recommendations.

### Phase 4 — Ongoing Refinement
- AI asks one deepening question per week in chat
- Answers automatically update the profile
- Brain dumps are mined for profile signals
- Profile completion percentage shown in dashboard
